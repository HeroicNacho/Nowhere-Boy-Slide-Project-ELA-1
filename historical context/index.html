Nowhere Boy Slide Project

ELA project on the book Nowhere Boy by Katherine Marsh.

Historical Context of Nowhere Boy by Katherine Marsh:

The setting of Nowhere Boy by Katherine Marsh is in 2015-2016, whereas it talks about problems that were more prominent in the past, such as in the 1950s.

For example, there is discrimination against Muslims such as Ahmed, because there are a few Muslim "terrorists" (who might not even be terrorists after all) and because of that, everyone thinks that Muslims are all 'bad' and there is major discrimination going on against them.
